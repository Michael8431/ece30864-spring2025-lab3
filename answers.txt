What are some Pros and Cons of Agile Development (vs non-Agile)? List 2 of each. 
Pros of Working with Agile:
1. Allows for adding of constant value to the customer.
2. Allows for constant collaboration with customers.
Cons of Working with Agile:
1. Uses smaller cross functional teams.
2. More resilient to scope changes in the Project.

Role and responsibilities of Scrum Master vs Product Owner
A SCRUM master is in charge of all the SCRUM related tasks such as tooling and facilitating sprints and meetings.
A product Owner is in chareg of the general direction of the team and handles release management as well as stakeholders.

Differentiate between Epic, Story, Bug, and tasks
Epic is the largest block of issue that exists and can be broken down directly by stories.
A story is the next one and is a system requirement that is expressed in a few short sentences. These can be broken down into tasks.
Tasks or subtasks are specific implementation of a story and are the second smallest block and cannot be broken apart.
Bugs are technically the smallest issues that exist to block certain tasks and by extensions stories.
